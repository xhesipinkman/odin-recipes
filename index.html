Fluffy Blueberry

Ingredients
  • 1 cup cake flour
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • salt, a pinch
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • Butter, for cooking
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
Instructions
  1. Sift together flour, sugar, salt, baking powder, and baking soda in a large bowl.
  2. Beat eggs with buttermilk and melted butter separately.
  3. Barely combine wet and dry ingredients to form a lumpy batter.
  4. Let the batter sit for 10-15 minutes.
  5. Melt butter over medium heat.
  6. Ladle 1/3 cup of batter on to the pan, and sprinkle with blueberries.
  7. Flip pancakes when bubbles appear on the surface and edges begin to brown - about 2-3 minutes per side.
